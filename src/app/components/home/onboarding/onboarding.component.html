WHAT IS MAKERSHARE?

In 2005, Make: introduced to the world a simple idea: We are all Makers. Makers integrate science and technology with art to create a world that is more productive, creative and inclusive. We unite around one idea: any ordinary object can be transformed into an innovation. Intel is a technology giant whose products empower us to change the future. Intel asks simply: What will you make? With Maker Share, Make: and Intel come together to support collaboration between Makers around the world.
